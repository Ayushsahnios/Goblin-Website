Procrastination Goblin

Your Cute but Ruthless Taskmaster Awaits!

Procrastination Goblin is here to keep you on track and hold you accountable like never before. Our app turns productivity into a game, mixing fun with relentless accountability to ensure you hit your goals—no excuses.

Key Features:

  • Task Tracking with Consequences: Set tasks and receive relentless reminders, escalating penalties, and even public social consequences if you miss deadlines.
  • Embarrassing Post Penalties: Miss a deadline, and the Goblin will post an embarrassing photo to your social media—no backing out!
  • Hilarious Spam Alerts: From relentless texts to surprise phone calls, the Goblin keeps you on your toes with comical but aggressive reminders until your tasks are done.
  • Guardrails Against Cheating: No shortcuts here—progress is real, and there's no gaming the system.
  • Bet on Productivity & Win: Place bets for or against your friends’ productivity. Win real money as you stay engaged, with financial incentives keeping you active and accountable.
